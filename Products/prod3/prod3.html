Invented in 1974 by Ernő Rubik, the original color-matching puzzle toy- the Rubik’s Cube- was created to help students understand three-dimensional problems. The prototype Magic Cube did things that the world had not seen before. It turned, it twisted and yet it did not break. Adding 54 colorful stickers to the six sides gave the puzzle its iconic look. Over 40 years of history has led the Rubik’s Cube to become one of the best-selling toys ever.
